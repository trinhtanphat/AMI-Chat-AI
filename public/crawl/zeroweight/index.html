FAQ

Why do I need a subscription if inference is free?

The subscription gives you continuous access to the avatar model, updates, and platform. Inference is not billed per minute to keep usage simple and predictable.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You lose access to the model and AI studio. Your avatar can be still used for inference.

Can I upgrade or downgrade plans?

Yes. You can switch plans anytime to unlock higher resolution or features.

What is a 2D Talking Avatar?

A 2D talking avatar is a digital character powered by AI that can move, react, and lip-sync to audio in real time. Zero Weight AI provides the technology to easily embed these virtual characters directly onto your website.

Can I create my own Anime Waifu?

Yes! With our Starter and Pro plans, you can upload your own custom character designs to create a unique Anime Waifu tailored specifically for your brand or interactive experience.
